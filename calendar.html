SPIS Closing Ceremony

Parents (as well as general public) are welcome to attend the SPIS closing ceremony. Parents/guests are advised to follow the information below to better navigate themselves inside the campus and to park their car.

  • Student project demos will be held in B230/240 of the CSE (Computer Science and Engineering) building. Please enter the lobby of the CSE building and take the elevator to go down to basement. The closing ceremony will be held in CSE 1202 in the CSE building.
  • The nearest location for parking is Hopkins Parking Structure. Please use the directions and maps to locate the Hopkins Parking Structure or other parking spaces. Payment stations are located at each parking lot and structure.
